How It Works

01

Wear the Sensor

Strap the device to your arm or knee using a simple velcro band. The sensor stays light and comfortable.

02

Track Your Movements

The ESP32 + IMU detect reps, angles, and motion accuracy in real time, displayed directly on the OLED.

03

View Live Dashboard

Connect through WiFi to check live data, adjust targets, and control your session from your phone.
