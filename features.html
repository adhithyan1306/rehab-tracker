Device Components

ESP32 Core

The brain of your device. Handles sensor reading, OLED UI, WiFi hosting, and button logic.

0.96” OLED Display

Displays reps, angles, targets, menus, and session details in a crisp neon UI.

MPU-6050 IMU

Tracks motion, angle, tilt, and movement precision with its accelerometer + gyroscope.

Active Buzzer

Audio feedback for reps, targets reached, and menu navigation signals.

Tactile Buttons

UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT & SELECT — navigate menus and control the device easily.
